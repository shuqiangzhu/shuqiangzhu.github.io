歡迎: 我是祝书强

About me: I am currrent an associate professor in School of Mathematics at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE) in Sichuan, China.

I completed my BSc and MSc degrees from Sichuan University under the guidance of Professor Shiqing Zhang. I then did my Ph.D. at University of Victoria in BC, Canada, under the guidance of Professor Florin Diacu.

Prior to the position of SWUFE, I worked at University of Science and Technology of China (中国科学技术大学). I also has been a visiting scholar at ITAM, where I was fortunate to work with Professor Ernesto Pérez-Chavela.

Research: My current research is centred around:

  1. Central configurations of the N-body problem, including that in the Euclidean space and space of constant curvature
  2. Expansive motions in the Newtonian N-body problem
  3. N-vortex problem
  4. Periodic motions for N-body problem in space of constant curvature

Teaching: I have taught Calculus, Ordinary Differential Equations, Real Analysis and Linear Algebra. This term, I am teaching Real Analysis, and a research-oriented undergraduate course themed around the N-body problem.
